Section D / AI Sentiments Dashboard Impact

Better feedback quality enabled better AI insight generation.

The success of the AI layer is directly linked to the improved quality of Talent Hive comments.

Without richer written comments, the AI dashboard would not have been able to identify meaningful patterns, themes, and insights. In FY26, better input quality made the AI layer possible, and early user feedback suggests the output is already creating practical value.
Why feedback quality matters for AI The AI Sentiments Dashboard works because the underlying feedback is detailed enough to analyze. The 180-character threshold led users to provide substantially richer written comments than expected.
Adoption and satisfaction story Since launch, the AI dashboard has received 68 pieces of user feedback, including 59 approvals, resulting in an 86.8% approval rate, a strong early signal of usefulness and satisfaction.
Users are responding well Early feedback indicates strong satisfaction among CRTs, career coaches, and other users. This reinforces that the investment in better-quality written feedback is already generating downstream value.
AI outputs became possible The dashboard can now convert qualitative feedback into themes, patterns, and actionable insights used in the broader review and talent process.
Key takeaway: FY26 Talent Hive enhancements improved the quality of feedback without reducing request demand. Users consistently provided substantially more written context than required, creating the foundation needed for the AI Sentiments Dashboard to generate meaningful insights. The main challenge remains completion, but the design changes have already created measurable value for CRTs, career coaches, and related talent stakeholders.
